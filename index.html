Privacy Policy

For: BiPro
Data Controller: Treffix
Effective Date: August 15, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and/or share your information when you use the BiPro application (“Application”). By using the Application, you agree to these privacy practices.

We comply with applicable laws and regulations, including Law No. 27 of 2022 on Personal Data Protection (PDP Law) and Apple’s platform guidelines.

1. Application Overview

BiPro is a cloud-based HR application designed to support employee needs, including attendance (with facial recognition), leave/permission requests, reimbursement claims, status notifications, performance monitoring, and scheduling.

2. Data We Process

The categories of data we may process depend on the features you use and your company’s configuration:

  • Identity & Account Data: name, email, phone number, employee ID, division, position.
  • Biometric Data (Face): facial images and/or representations (embeddings/templates) for attendance verification. By default, verification is processed on-device; template storage on the server only applies if enabled by your company.
  • Attendance & Location Data: clock-in/clock-out times, IP address, approximate or precise location (optional depending on company settings) for attendance validation.
  • Request Data: leave/permission requests and history; reasons, attachments if any.
  • Reimbursement Data: claim details, amount, supporting documents (receipt photos/files).
  • Performance Data: performance metrics/assessments displayed in the app as provided by your company.
  • Photos/Media & Camera: camera/gallery access when capturing/uploading photos for face verification or attaching documents.
  • Push Notifications: device tokens to send request status updates and announcements.
  • Device & Diagnostic Data: device model, OS version, crash logs, and performance data to maintain app stability.

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Provide core features: attendance (including face verification), leave/permission requests, reimbursement, schedules, and performance display.
  • Employee account authentication and management.
  • Send push notifications related to statuses and company announcements.
  • Technical/diagnostic analytics and service quality improvement.
  • Compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process data based on: (i) consent (e.g., camera/location access, biometrics); (ii) performance of employment/service agreements with your company; (iii) legitimate interests to operate and secure the Application; and/or (iv) legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Your Company (employer) as the controller/party responsible for HR operations.
  • Service Providers (processors) such as cloud hosting, databases, push notification delivery (Apple Push Notification service/APNs), and crash reporting/technical analytics. These providers are bound by data processing agreements and confidentiality obligations.

6. Cross-Border Transfers

Since BiPro is cloud-based, data may be processed/transferred to other jurisdictions. We ensure adequate protection in accordance with the PDP Law and applicable regulations.

7. Retention & Security

  • Data is retained as long as the account is active or as needed for the purposes outlined, unless required longer by law or your company’s policy.
  • We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption in transit, role-based access control, audits) to protect data.
  • No method is 100% secure; we continuously improve our security standards.

8. Data Subject Rights

You have the right to access, correct, update, object, restrict, withdraw consent, transfer, or request deletion of your personal data to the extent permitted by law. Requests may be submitted via the contact below or through your company’s HR administrator.

9. Children

The Application is intended for employee (adult) use and is not designed for individuals under 18 years old.

10. Device Permissions

  • Camera & Photos: for face verification and uploading reimbursement evidence.
  • Location (optional): for attendance validation according to company policy.
  • Notifications: to receive status updates and announcements.

You can manage permissions through your device settings.

11. Tracking & Advertising

BiPro does not engage in cross-app/website tracking for advertising, does not use IDFA, and does not serve targeted ads. If tracking becomes necessary in the future, we will comply with the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework and obtain your consent first.

12. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via updates on this page and/or through the Application. The effective date will be updated at the top.
